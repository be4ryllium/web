Magazine Cover

         This is the cover for the fictional magazine I created, Plain Sight. The background image is an ocean sunset in La Jolla, as stated on the cover. I decided to use this image because it evokes a sense of tranquility in the viewer.

         The magazine itself is about nature photography, which is the reason why the background image fits in with it. I added more article previews to it which are also about nature.

Photoshop skills used:
  • Masking (masking out some parts of the text to make it blend with the water)
  • Brush tool (darkening some parts of the image to make the text blend in more)
