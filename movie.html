Movie Poster

         This is my movie poster for the movie Oppenheimer: Arms Race, which is a supposed sequel to the Oppenheimer movie. This movie would show Oppenheimer dealing with the consequences of the atomic bomb more than the original during the Cold War. Oppenheimer is standing in the middle of the two sides because he was a communist while also supporting the United States.

         In the image of me in the center, I actually just have normal clothes on and I had to mask on a picture of a suit and a hat onto me, which I think turned out pretty okay. I used separate layers with different blend modes to create more realistic lighting and to blend images together.

Photoshop skills used:
  • Masking (masking out the suit and hat to put on me, as well as myself out of the image)
  • Brush tool (darkening / changing the color of some parts of the image to create a nice gradient effect)
  • Blend modes (creating realistic lighting on parts of my body and background objects)
  • Gradient tool (creating an actual gradient between the two sides)
