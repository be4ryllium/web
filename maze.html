Haunted Maze Flyer

         This is a flyer for Skills USA's haunted maze. I created this flyer around halloween time to advertise for the event. The background image is a picture I took somewhere in San Diego by a lake. I decided to use this image because it was a cloudy day and it had potential as a scary image.

         I then used Generative AI to insert a haunted house at the top of the hill. I also used it to generate a bat next to the house. I then inserted a Google Maps view of the venue and text advertising the event, as well as an evil blue emoji to set the tone of the flyer.

Photoshop skills used:
  • Generative AI (for the haunted house and bat)
  • A Layer Style (to add a stroke to the text)
