Album Cover

         This is the album cover I created for my album called "Adrift". I took this picture in Point Loma, San Diego because I liked the scene of the crashed boat against the rocks. When choosing the background picture for my album cover, I chose this image because it evoked a sense of loneliness which I thought would work well in the context of an album.

         When working with this image on photoshop, I decided to make the main focus of it, the boat, be the only thing in the image that isn't in black and white in order to draw more attention to it.

Photoshop skills used:
  • Adjustment layer (for the black and white effect)
  • Masking (masking out the black and white filter around the boat)
  • Layer style (for the text stroke effect)
