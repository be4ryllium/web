Halloween Scene

         This is a scary image I created during halloween. The background image is of a road in the mountains that I took a picture of when going to San Diego. The clouds were on top of the mountains at the time, which makes it look foggy and mysterious. I layered on an image of me walking forward to make it look like I am jogging down the road.

         In photoshop, I used the brush tool to create a shadow around me to more seemlessly blend me in with the background. I also used it to create tentacles coming out of the darkness that it looks like I am running away from. I added eyes to the top right corner of the image to add to the creepiness.

Photoshop skills used:
  • Masking (masking me out of the image I was in)
  • Brush tool (for the darkness and tentacles)
